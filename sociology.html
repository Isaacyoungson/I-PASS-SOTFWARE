      3.Contemporary Social Problems. (this course provides the study of current social problems. emphasis is based on causes, consequences, and possible solutions to problems associated with families, schools, workplaces, comunities, and the environment.)
      3. Social Diversity (this course provides the study of diverse roles, interests, opportunities, contributions, and experiences in social life. topics in this course includes: race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, class and religion.)
