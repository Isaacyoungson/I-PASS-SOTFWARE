COURSE OUTLINE

    • 1. Elementary German (this course introduces the fundamental elements of the German language. topics in this course is based on the development of basic listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.)
      2. Intermidiate German (this course is the continuation of "elementary German". topics in this course is based on the development of basic listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills.)
      3. Advanced German (this course provide a review and expansion of the essential skills of the German Language. emphasis is based on the study of authentic and representative literary and cultural context.)
