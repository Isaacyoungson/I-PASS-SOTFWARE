COURSE OUTLINE

    • 1.Introduction to English Language (this course is to introduce students to english as a subject on its own.)
      2.English Writing (this topic introduces research techniques, documentation styles, writing strategies.)
      3.Literature (this topic is literature-based research and documentation. it includes: poetry, prose, drama, plot, characterization, theme, cultural context.)
      4. American Literature I (this course covers the selected works in American literature from beginnings to 1865. its emphasis is on the historical background, cultural context, and literary abalysis of selected prose, poetry and drama.)
      5. American Literature II (this course covers the american literature from 1865 to the present. it emphasis on the historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama.)
      6. Major American Writers (this course cover the work of several major American authors. it emphasis on American history, culture and the literary merits.)
      7. British Literature I (this course covers the selected works in British literature from the beginnings to the Romantic period. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      8. British Literature II (this course covers the selected works in British literature from the Romantic period to the present. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      9. Major British Writers (this course cover the work of several major American authors. it emphasis on American history, culture and the literary merits.)
      10. Western World Literature I (this course survey the selected European works from the Classical period through the Renaissance. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      11. Western World Literature II (this course covers the selected European works from the Neoclassical period to the present. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      12. World Literature I (this course covers the selected works from the Pacific, Asia, African, Europe and the Americas from their literary beginnings through the seventeenth century. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      13. World Literature II (this course covers the selected works from the Pacific, Asia, Africa, Europe and the Americas from the eighteenth century to the present. it emphasis on historical background, cultural context, and literary analysis of selected prose, poetry and drama)
      14. Southern Literature (this course covers the analytical study of the works of several Southern authors. it emphasis on the historical and cultural contexts, themes, aesthetic features of individual works, biographical background of the authors.)
      15. African American Literature (this course provide survey of the development of African-American literature from its beginnings to the present. it emphasis on historical and cultural context, themes, literary traditions, and backgrounds of the authors.)
      16. Literature by Women (this course provide an analytical study of the work of several women authors. it emphasis on the historical and cultural contexts, themes, biographical backgrounds of the authors.)
