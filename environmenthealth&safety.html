COURSE OUTLINE

    • 1. Occupational Safety (topics in this course includes: accident investigation, workers compensation, record keeping, training, machine guarding, facilities, personal protection, and fire protection.)
      2. INdustrial Hygiene (topics in this course includes: hazard recognition, health standards, air sampling, ventilation, noise exposure, and temperature stress.)
      3. Electrical Safety (topics in this course includes: controlling hazards in the workplace, recognizing electrical hazards, understanding ground paths, and interpreting electrical standards.)
      4. OSHA Regulations (topics in this course includes: hazard communication, blood-borne pathogens, and the laboratory standards)
      5. environmental Management
      6. Environmental Health and Toxicology (this course covers the effects of environmental agents on human health. emphasis is based on the principles of toxicology and federal regulations relevant to environmental health.)
      7. Industrial Hygiene Sampling (topics in this course includes: the calibration and operation of sampling equipment and instruments.)
      8. Incident Management (topics in this course includes : analysis and application of the incident command system from the discovery of a hazardous substance to release decontaminated and termination procedures.)
      9. Occupational Ergonomics (topics in this course includes: anthropometry, working postures, task analysis, manual material handling, lifting equations, and environmental factors.)
      10. Radiation Protection (this course covers theory, detection, health effects and regulation of ionizing radiation. emphasis is based on the compliance with federal regulations in the occupational settings.)
