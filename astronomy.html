COURSE OUTLINE

    • 1. General Astronomy I (topics in this course is based on the introduction to the science of modern astronomy with concentration on the solar system. emphasis is based on the history and physics of astronomy and an introduction to the solar system including the planets, comets, and meteors.)
      2. General Astronomy I Lab (this course is a laboratory to accompany General astronomy I. emphasis is based on laboratory experiences which enhance the materials presented in the theory aspect.)
      3. General Astronomy II (this is the continuation with emphasis beyond the solar system. topics in this course includes the sun, stars, galaxies, and the large universe including cosmology.)
      4. General Astronomy II Lab (this course is a laboratory to accompany General astronomy II. emphasis is based on laboratory experiences which enhance the materials presented in the theory aspect.)
