COURSE OUTLINE

    • 1. Basic Biological Processes. (this course introduces the chemistry importance to biological processes.)
      2.General Chemistry 1. (topics in this course includes: measurement, atomic and molecular structure, periodicity, chemical reactions, chemical Bonding, stiochiometry, thermochemistry, gas laws, solutions.)
      3.General Chemistry 2. (topics in this course includes: kinetics, equilibrium, ionic and redox equations, acid-base theory, electrochemistry,thermodynamics, introduction to nuclear and organic chemistry, complex ions.)
      4.Organic Chemistry 1 (topics in this course includes: nomenclature, structure, properties , reaction and mechanism of hydrocarbons, alkyl halides, alcohols and ethers, isomerization, stereochemistry, spectroscopy.)
      5.Organic Chemistry 2 (topics in this course includes: nomeclature, structure properties, reactions and mechanism of aromatis, aldehydes, ketones, carboxylic acids and derivatives, amines and heterocyclics, multi-step synthesis emphasized.)
      6.Analytical Chemistry
      7.Biochemical Principle (topics in this course includes: structure, properties, reactions and mechanisms of biomacromolecules including amino acids, peptides, proteins, carbohydrates and nuclear acids, enzymatic metabolic pathways, biochemical genetics.)
